Agapic Chants

An invitation to sing together

Agapic Chants are simple, singable repetitions drawn from real human struggles. They do what sacred music has always done: bring us into our bodies, move what is stuck, connect us to each other and to something larger. Without requiring belief in anything except the experience itself.

This chantbook is a living commons — recordings, lyrics, chords, and sheet music, freely available to anyone who wants to sing.
