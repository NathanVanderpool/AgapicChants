How to use
these chants

These chants were written to be sung — in living rooms, circles, hospitals, protests, retreats, anywhere people gather. They belong to everyone who sings them.

What's free

  • Sing them anywhere, in any context
  • Teach them in classes, workshops, or ceremonies
  • Record them and share the recording
  • Adapt the melody, words, or arrangement
  • Use them in non-commercial therapeutic or spiritual work
  • Use them in commercial products or paid platforms without asking first
  • Use them without telling people where they came from

Commercial use

If you're making money from something that includes these chants — an app, a paid course, a commercial recording, a branded event — please get in touch first. We can usually find an arrangement that works for everyone.

The goal isn't to restrict the music. It's to make sure that if someone is building commercial value on top of this work, the person who made it is part of that conversation.
